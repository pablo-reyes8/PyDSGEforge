# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in PyDSGEforge a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, precise, and constructive.
- Welcome questions and differing technical viewpoints.
- Critique ideas and evidence, never people.
- Protect private information and acknowledge contributions.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, trolling, and
publishing another person's private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately using the channel in `SECURITY.md`.
Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, commits, code, issues,
and other contributions that violate this policy, and may temporarily or
permanently ban contributors when appropriate. Reports will be handled as
confidentially as practical.

This policy is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html).
